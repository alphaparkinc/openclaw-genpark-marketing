# genpark-marketing

## Overview
This skill provides comprehensive marketing support, from idea generation and ad copywriting to structuring scalable growth experiments. It mimics the capabilities of a digital marketing agency account manager.

## Capabilities
1. **Ad Copywriting**: Generate multi-variant ad copy (Facebook, TikTok, Google Ads) tailored to different audience personas.
2. **Growth Experiments**: Create structured A/B testing plans for landing pages and marketing channels.
3. **Trend Analysis**: Leverage current social trends to propose viral marketing concepts.

## Usage Instructions
When a user requests a marketing plan:
- Request their target audience, budget constraints, and primary marketing channel.
- Always output at least three distinct angles (e.g., Emotional, Functional, FOMO).
- Format growth experiments into clear hypotheses, metrics to track, and execution steps.
